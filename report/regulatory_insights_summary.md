# Regulatory Insights Summary

## Overview
This report analyses regulatory data to identify key trends, risk themes, and compliance impact areas.
The objective is to support stakeholders with clear, data-driven insights.

## Key Findings
- Increase in regulatory updates across financial services
- High-risk themes concentrated in compliance and reporting
- Notable variation across regions and categories

## Tools Used
- SQL for data extraction
- Excel for data cleaning and validation
- Tableau for dashboard visualisation

## Business Impact
- Improved visibility into regulatory risk
- Faster decision-making through dashboards
- Actionable insights for compliance teams
